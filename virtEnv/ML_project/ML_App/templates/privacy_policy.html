Privacy Policy

Your Trust, Our Commitment

Last Updated: February 28, 2025

1. Introduction

At EduLearn, we value your privacy and are dedicated to safeguarding your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your data while you engage with our cutting-edge online learning platform (the "Service"). By using EduLearn, you agree to the practices described herein.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information to provide and enhance your learning experience:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, and password when you register as a student or instructor.
  • Payment Information: Securely processed billing details for course enrollments.
  • Usage Data: Interaction details such as courses viewed, time spent, and your IP address.
  • Uploaded Content: Materials like assignments or profile images you submit.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information helps us deliver a personalized and secure experience:

  • Customize and improve the Service.
  • Process payments and manage accounts.
  • Send updates, course recommendations, and promotions.
  • Protect the platform’s security and integrity.

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell your data. It may be shared with:

  • Service Providers: Trusted partners for payment processing, hosting, or analytics (e.g., Stripe, Google Analytics).
  • Instructors: Minimal data (e.g., your name) for course management.
  • Legal Obligations: When required by law or to protect our community.

5. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to optimize your experience and analyze usage. Manage preferences via your browser settings.

6. Data Security

We employ industry-standard measures to protect your data. While we strive for security, no system is entirely risk-free.

7. Your Rights

You may have rights to:

  • Access, update, or delete your data.
  • Opt out of marketing emails.
  • Request data portability.

Contact us at privacy@edulearn.com to exercise these rights.

8. Third-Party Links

Our Service may link to external sites. We’re not responsible for their privacy practices—please review their policies.

9. Children’s Privacy

EduLearn is not designed for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children and will delete it if discovered.

10. Policy Updates

We may revise this policy, posting updates here with a new "Last Updated" date. Check back regularly.
